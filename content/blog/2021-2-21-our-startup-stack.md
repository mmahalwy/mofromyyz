Here's a look at our operational and technical infrastructure — the tools and services that power our startup.

## Business Tools

- **Banking:** Brex
- **Equity Management:** Carta
- **Product Management:** Linear.app
- **Data Management:** Airtable
- **Design:** Figma
- **Documentation:** Notion
- **Team Communication:** Slack
- **Email Services:** Google and Superhuman
- **Scheduling:** Calendly
- **Source Control:** GitHub
- **Code Editor:** VS Code with Tabnine
- **File Management:** Filestack and Uploadcare
- **Email Campaigns:** Sendgrid

## Tech Stack

### Frontend

- **Hosting:** Vercel/Next.js
- **Framework:** Next.js with React
- **UI Components:** Ant Design
- **API Client:** Apollo GraphQL

### Backend

- **Deployment:** Heroku
- **Runtime:** Node.js
- **Framework:** Koa
- **Database ORM:** Sequelize
- **API:** GraphQL
- **Error Monitoring:** Sentry

We use extensive GraphQL tooling, testing frameworks, linting utilities, and TypeScript for type safety throughout the stack.
